# 📊 Shopify Funnel Optimization Case Study

## 🚀 Overview
This project analyzes a Shopify DTC brand generating $50K+ monthly revenue to identify funnel leaks, optimize conversions, and uncover revenue growth opportunities.

---

## 🎯 Objective
- Identify revenue leaks in the funnel
- Improve conversion rate
- Increase repeat purchases
- Optimize checkout & payment experience

---

## 📁 Dataset
- 7,400+ transaction records  
- Fields included:
  - Customer ID  
  - Order Number  
  - Product Type  
  - Payment Gateway  
  - Revenue Metrics  
  - Location Data  

---

## 🔍 Key Insights
- 🚨 54% of customers are one-time buyers (retention gap)  
- 🔁 Repeat Purchase Rate: 46%  
- 📉 Purchase Frequency: 1.68  
- 🌍 Strong sales concentration in key regions (California, Texas, New York)  
- 🛍️ Few products contribute majority of revenue  

---

## 🚨 Problems Identified
- Low customer retention  
- Inconsistent conversion performance  
- Heavy reliance on top المنتجات/products  
- Lack of customer segmentation  
- Limited lifecycle marketing strategy  

---

## 💡 Solutions Implemented / Recommended
- Funnel optimization (landing → checkout)  
- Email & retention marketing strategies  
- Product bundling & upsell techniques  
- Customer segmentation (high vs low LTV)  
- Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO) improvements  

---

## 📈 Results & Impact
- Identified key conversion leaks affecting funnel performance  
- Highlighted opportunity to improve conversion rate by **15–20%**  
- Analyzed customer behavior revealing **46% repeat purchase rate**  
- Discovered retention gap with **54% one-time buyers**  
- Estimated **15%+ revenue growth potential** through funnel optimization  

---

## 🛠 Tools Used
- Power BI  
- Excel  
- Shopify Data  

---

## 🧠 Key Takeaway
Small improvements in conversion and retention can unlock significant revenue growth without increasing ad spend.

---

## 🚀 Strategic Recommendations
- Implement email & SMS retention flows  
- Optimize product pages for higher conversions  
- Introduce bundles & cross-sells  
- Improve checkout experience to reduce drop-offs
